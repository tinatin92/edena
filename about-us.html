We have a very large selection of pickles, sauces, spices, vegetables
LLC Edena (Terjoli canning factory) has been operating since 2010 and is mainly engaged in canning production. Our main direction is the processing of wood of different colors and types and the production of wood pulp. Under the brand name "MADLI" we produce and sell pepper, jonjoli and garlic pickles, beans, vegetable breakfast, adjika, various fruit jams and natural syrups in jars.
